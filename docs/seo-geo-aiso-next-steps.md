# Orvyn SEO, GEO, and AI Search Next Steps

This checklist covers the manual work to complete after deploying the SEO-ready build to `https://orvyn.cc`.

## Google Search Console

1. Verify the `https://orvyn.cc` property.
2. Submit the sitemap:
   `https://orvyn.cc/sitemap.xml`
3. Inspect these URLs:
   - `https://orvyn.cc/`
   - `https://orvyn.cc/services`
   - `https://orvyn.cc/about`
   - `https://orvyn.cc/case-studies`
   - `https://orvyn.cc/contact`
4. Request indexing after the new deployment is live.
5. Recheck sitemap status after Google has had time to crawl it.

## Bing Webmaster Tools

1. Add `https://orvyn.cc`.
2. Submit `https://orvyn.cc/sitemap.xml`.
3. Inspect the same five public URLs listed above.

## Content And Case Study Plan

The PDFs are useful, but HTML pages are easier for search engines and AI engines to understand.

Future case study pages should include:

- Project background
- Problem or opportunity
- Direction
- Execution
- Visuals
- Services involved
- Outcome, only when real and approved

Do not add fake metrics, fake reviews, fake testimonials, or unverified client results.

## GEO / AI Search Plan

To help generative AI engines understand and recommend Orvyn:

- Keep the Orvyn description consistent across profiles.
- Earn mentions from client websites, social posts, directories, and partner pages.
- Publish clear service pages.
- Turn project decks into HTML case studies.
- Keep structured data accurate.
- Avoid fake claims, exaggerated results, or hidden schema.
