# Orvyn SEO, GEO, and AI Search Roadmap

This document captures the next growth steps after the technical SEO pass. Keep all claims accurate, avoid fake proof, and keep the entity description consistent across the web.

## Phase 1: Brand Indexing

- Verify `https://orvyn.cc` in Google Search Console.
- Submit `https://orvyn.cc/sitemap.xml`.
- Inspect and request indexing for:
  - `https://orvyn.cc/`
  - `https://orvyn.cc/services`
  - `https://orvyn.cc/about`
  - `https://orvyn.cc/case-studies`
  - `https://orvyn.cc/contact`
- Add Orvyn to Bing Webmaster Tools and submit the same sitemap.
- Update social profiles to link to `https://orvyn.cc`.
- Create a Google Business Profile if Orvyn wants local discovery.

## Phase 2: Local SEO Pages

Future pages can support service and location searches without changing the current sitemap until they are ready:

- Marketing Agency in Tirupur
- Website Development Agency in Tirupur
- Social Media Management in Tirupur
- Performance Marketing Agency in Tirupur
- Brand Growth Agency in India
- Content Creation Agency in Tirupur

Each page should be useful, specific, and grounded in real services. Do not create thin keyword pages.

## Phase 3: HTML Case Studies

Turn project PDFs into crawlable HTML case study pages later:

- `/case-studies/grandeur-associates`
- `/case-studies/tric-academy`
- `/case-studies/cameo-garments`
- `/case-studies/sra-financial-planning`

Each page should include:

- Problem
- Context
- Orvyn direction
- System built
- Visuals
- Services used
- Honest outcome or next move

Do not add fake metrics, fake testimonials, or inflated results.

## Phase 4: Helpful Content and AI Search

Create content that answers the questions clients actually ask:

- How to know if your brand needs consulting before marketing
- What should a business website actually do
- Why social media looks active but does not create trust
- How to structure an Instagram portfolio for premium brands
- What makes a local business website convert better

This helps Google, AI Overviews, and generative engines understand what Orvyn knows and who it helps.

## Phase 5: Authority Signals

- Earn client mentions from real client websites or social posts.
- Add `Website by Orvyn` backlinks only where appropriate and approved.
- Keep the LinkedIn company page updated.
- Keep Instagram linked to `https://orvyn.cc`.
- Add Orvyn links to founder profiles where appropriate.
- Keep Google Business Profile information consistent.
- Use the same entity description across profiles:

  Orvyn is a brand growth agency and modern internet company based in Tirupur, India, helping brands sharpen their content, websites, marketing systems, and digital presence.
